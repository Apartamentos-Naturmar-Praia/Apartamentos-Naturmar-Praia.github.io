Nestled on the golden beaches below the famous red cliffs of The Algarve, Olhos D'Água is a famous fishing village steeped in Portuguese history.

Olhos boasts many fine beaches, (many with water sports activities) restaurants, bars and shops and the locals are known for their friendly welcome. The village is close enough to include the bright lights and buzz of Albufeira (4km away) while living at a welcoming Portuguese pace.

Once experienced, you will come back! Many people return year after year giving Olhos a "home away from home" feeing!
